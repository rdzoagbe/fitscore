# P7C Copy QA Checklist

Use this before showing the site publicly.

## Public pages

- The homepage headline explains the outcome clearly.
- CTAs say what happens next.
- The site avoids vague phrases like “powered by AI” as the main selling point.
- Pricing does not imply live payment if Stripe is not configured.
- LinkedIn copy is paste-only and privacy-first.

## App pages

- Dashboard tells the user what to do next.
- Analyzer uses “check match” or “run analysis” consistently.
- CV Coach uses “improve” and “tailor” language.
- Saved items use “career history” consistently.
- Error messages are human-readable.

## Trust

- No copy asks for LinkedIn password.
- No copy promises interviews or job offers.
- No copy says the app bypasses ATS systems.
- Privacy and data deletion language is visible.

## Mobile

- Headings do not wrap awkwardly.
- CTA buttons are readable and at least 44px high.
- Long paragraphs are not cramped.
- Cards have enough spacing.
